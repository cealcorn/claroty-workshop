## GOALS
- Locate and investigate Insights within CTD
- Understand how Insights differ from Alerts and Vulnerabilities
- Explore Insight details and supporting information
---
## TASKS
1. Navigate to `Risks & Vulnerabilities` > `Insights`.
2. Review the Insights page and note:
   - Total number of Insights
   - Insight categories
   - Insight severities
   - Insight statuses
3. Explore the available filters and columns.
4. Select an Insight and review its details.
5. Select the drop down menu on an asset listed within an Insight and review the information in its columns.
6. Using the information that you learned from that asset, now open a related Alert and Vulnerability and compare the information available in each:
   - What triggered the item?
   - What assets are involved?
   - What actions are available?
   - What level of investigation is supported?
---
