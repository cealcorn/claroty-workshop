## GOALS
- Learn how to approach an unknown OT environment using CTD
- Develop a high-level understanding of the network
- Identify areas that require additional investigation
- Understand how visibility data supports risk assessments
---
## TASKS
1. Navigate to `Visibility` > `Overview`.
2. Using information avaiable on the **Visibility Overview**, **Assets**, and **Zones** pages, determine:
   - The total number of assets
   - The ratio of IT to OT to IoT assets
   - The total number of zones
   - The most populous zones
   - Which zones and assets conduct the higher number of communications
---
## REFLECTION
- What types of systems appear to exist within the environment?
- Which zones appear most critical to operations?
- What information can be learned from topology views that is difficult to identify from asset lists?
- How would you determine which assets are the highest priority for investigation?
- How can you use the filter options to filter out noise?
